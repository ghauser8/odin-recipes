Matty Matheson's Beef Lasagna

Matty Mathson is a Canadian chef from Toronto who is redefining popular cuisine and celebrity chef-dom for a generation of Americans, Canadians, and now Britons. If you watch his cooking shows you'll get a sense for how he's breaking about every norm there is when it comes to presenting food. You might also notice that he's a fan of american cheese, parsely stems, and other down-to-earth ingredients and methods that appeal to both your pragmatic and unfeigned sides. He has the professional pedigree to back up his recipes, but ignore that and try them if you want to understand the Matheson approach.
